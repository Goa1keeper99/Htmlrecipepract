LasagnaRecipe

This is how you make lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ground Beef

Instructions

  1. Cook the beef to golden brown
  2. Boil water and put the noodles in
  3. Layer the noodles, sauce, and beef in a 9x13 pan
  4. bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
